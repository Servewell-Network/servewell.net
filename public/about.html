About

Servewell.net exists to help people serve well. It begins with Scripture, through which we discover our identity, clarify our purpose, and move toward healthy relationships.

Servewell.net is for a mixed audience and is growing quickly. For the most current view of what is available, the Features page highlights the latest content and functionality.

Servewell.net is short for Servewell Network. Think of that as a network of people that help each other. This is not intended to be one of those websites where you see problems but have no way to offer something better. Please offer improvements and suggestions either on GitHub or increasingly through the features of Servewell.net itself, and let us know if you're willing to take credit for the changes or if you prefer anonymity. As spam increases, you may need to work harder to prove you're a real contributor of content, but don't give up! Only together can we experience God's good plan for our world.
